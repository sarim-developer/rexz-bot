## Dashboard Modules

The dashboard is divided into several easy-to-navigate pages, each tailored to specific functionality.

###  Anti-Nuke Settings
This is where you activate and configure RexZ's flagship feature. Toggle individual protections on or off:
- Channel Deletion/Creation Limits
- Role Modification Limits
- Ban/Kick Thresholds
- Webhook Creation Protections
Tune the strictness of the anti-nuke system to fit your server's specific needs.
• Configure your server using the dashboard → https://www.rexzbot.xyz

###  Trustables Management
Your server likely has trusted administrators. The **Trustables Page** allows you to assign specific users an exemption from the Anti-Nuke system. 
- Ensure your loyal staff won't trigger the anti-nuke when performing legitimate mass-mod actions.
- Easily add, view, and remove trusted users.

###  Tickets System
Manage your server's support infrastructure visually.
